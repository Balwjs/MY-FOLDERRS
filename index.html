Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A focused set of tools for keeping your important documents private, organised and accessible.

📂
Colour-Coded Folders

Organise files into nested folders with priority colour labels — red, yellow, blue, and grey — so you can find things at a glance.

📷
Document Scanner

Point your camera at any physical document. My Folder auto-crops, enhances, and saves a clean PDF instantly.

📄
PDF Tools

Merge, split, compress, OCR, and password-protect PDFs — all without an internet connection or subscription.

✍️
Sign & Annotate

Add your handwritten signature or annotate any PDF with PencilKit. Saved directly into the file — no third-party required.

🔐
Biometric Lock

Face ID and Touch ID protect the entire app. Per-document passwords add another layer using PBKDF2-SHA256 hashing.

📤
Share Extension

Save files directly from Safari, Mail, Files, or any app using the "Save to My Folder" option in the iOS share sheet.

How It Works

Up and running in seconds.

No setup, no sign-in, no nonsense.

1
Create a folder

Tap + on the home screen. Name it, pick a colour label, and your folder is ready.

2
Add documents

Import from the Files app or Photos, scan with your camera, or share directly from any other app.

3
Organise & secure

Rename, move, colour-prioritise, and password-protect individual files. Use PDF Tools to merge, split, or compress.

4
Sign & share

Open any PDF, sign it, annotate it, then share via the standard iOS share sheet — all without ever leaving the app.
